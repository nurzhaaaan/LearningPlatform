About TestPrep Academy

Specializing in IELTS, SAT, NUET, and UTO exam preparation since 2023

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide specialized, comprehensive test preparation that empowers students to achieve their highest potential scores on standardized exams.

We believe that with the right preparation, guidance, and practice, every student can excel in these important educational assessments and open doors to their dream institutions.

Our Vision

We envision becoming the premier test preparation platform in Kazakhstan and Central Asia, known for our exceptional success rates and comprehensive approach to exam preparation.

We aim to continuously innovate our teaching methodologies and adapt to changing examination patterns, ensuring our students are always ahead of the curve.

Our Specializations

IELTS Preparation

Comprehensive preparation for all four IELTS modules with specialized instructors for each component. Our students consistently achieve band scores of 7.0 and above.

SAT Preparation

Expert strategies and extensive practice for both Math and Evidence-Based Reading & Writing sections. Our students have achieved scores in the 90th percentile and higher.

NUET Preparation

Specialized coaching for the National University Entrance Test with subject-specific experts to help students gain admission to Kazakhstan's top universities.

UTO Preparation

Elite preparation for the Unified Testing of Olympiad participants, helping talented students showcase their abilities and secure special admissions and scholarships.
